Whip crack, always

Every slap plays a real whip crack. Because your MacBook should know who's in charge.

Agent mode

Presses Enter 80ms after impact. Your AI stops asking permission and just does the thing.

Warcraft mode

"Yes, me lord." "I can do that." "Work work." Your AI now responds like a WC3 peon assigned a task.

🎯

Menu bar app

One icon. Click Start, enter password once, never touch a terminal again.

⚡

Raw accelerometer

Reads the Bosch BMI286 IMU directly via IOKit HID. No lag, no polling, just physics.

INSTALL

1
Download SpankTheAgent.zip and unzip it.
2
Drag SpankTheAgent.app to Applications.
3
Open it. ⚡ appears in your menu bar.
4
Click → Start. Enter password once for accelerometer access.
5
Slap your MacBook. Done.
