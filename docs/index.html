Whip crack on every slap

Every time you hit your MacBook, a satisfying whip crack plays. Because your laptop should know who's in charge.

⌨️

Agent mode

Automatically presses Enter 80ms after the slap — confirming whatever your AI agent was nervously asking permission for.

⚔️

Warcraft mode

"Yes, me lord." "I can do that." "Work work." Your AI now responds like a WC3 peon who was just assigned a task.

🎯

Menu bar app

Lives quietly in your menu bar. Toggle Agent and Warcraft modes with one click. No terminal, no config files.

⚡

Apple Silicon accelerometer

Reads directly from the Bosch BMI286 IMU via IOKit HID — the same chip that protects your hard drive from drops.

🔓

Open source · MIT

Forked from spank by Tai Groot. Add your own sounds, tweak the sensitivity, do whatever you want.

How to install

1
Download SpankTheAgent.zip and unzip it.
2
Drag SpankTheAgent.app to Applications.
3
Open the app. A menu bar icon appears.
4
Click the icon → Start. Enter your password once — needed to access the accelerometer sensor.
5
Slap your MacBook. That's it.
Requirements: macOS 13+ · Apple Silicon (M2 or later)
